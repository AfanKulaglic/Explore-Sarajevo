# Saraya Connect Ecosystem — Complete Knowledge Base
> This document describes every user-facing feature, screen, workflow, and
business aspect of the entire Saraya Connect ecosystem. It covers the WiFi network
infrastructure, all digital platforms, the unified account and reward system,
advertising capabilities, and network locations. Written as a chatbot knowledge base
so the bot can answer any question about what Saraya Connect is, what users can
do, how things work, and how the products relate to each other.
---
## 1. What Is Saraya Connect?
**Saraya Connect** is an urban digital platform built by **Saraya Solutions** in
Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. It has two interconnected dimensions:
1. **A city-wide free WiFi network** covering the most frequented locations in
Sarajevo — from Baščaršija and Ferhadija to Skenderija and Bjelašnica. Every user
who connects to the network passes through a captive portal, which serves as both a
gateway to free internet and a high-visibility advertising space.
2. **A family of connected web applications** — including a quiz platform, a city
exploration guide, a tech editorial site, a game hub, and a rewards marketplace — all
linked through a single user account, shared virtual economy, and Single Sign-On
(SSO).
### The dual value proposition
- **For users**: Free internet access at key city locations, plus a digital ecosystem
where they can earn virtual coins by taking quizzes, exploring local businesses,
reading articles, and playing games — then redeem those coins for real rewards.
- **For advertisers/brands**: A guaranteed-attention advertising channel where every
WiFi user must see the captive portal content. No ad blockers, no skipping, no
scrolling past. The brand appears at the moment of highest user attention — when
they are actively connecting to the internet.
### What makes Saraya Connect unique
Unlike random digital ads that compete for attention in a crowded feed, Saraya
Connect captures user attention at a specific, privileged moment — when they are
actively connecting to WiFi. The captive portal is the only content on screen. The
user must see it before accessing the internet. This combination of a useful free
service and high-visibility advertising creates a win-win ecosystem that is rare in
Bosnia and Herzegovina's urban digital space.

## 2. The Saraya Connect WiFi Network
### Network overview
The Saraya Connect WiFi network covers **seven of Sarajevo's most frequented
and strategically important locations**. Each location was chosen based on foot
traffic volume, urban significance, and advertising visibility potential. Together, they
form a cohesive digital network that follows the flow of urban life in the city.
### Network locations
**1. Baščaršija — Sebilj area**
The heart of Sarajevo's old town and the city's most visited tourist and local hub.
Enormous daily foot traffic from tourists and residents alike. High mobile device
engagement.
**2. Sarači Street**
One of the busiest pedestrian streets in the city center, lined with cafés, restaurants,
and shops. High pedestrian flow throughout the entire day.
**3. Ferhadija Street — extending to Aria Mall**
The central pedestrian zone connecting the historic core with the modern urban
center. Covers both traditional and contemporary visitor profiles.
**4. Zeleni Beretki — Branilaca Sarajeva — Susan Sontag Square — National
Theatre**
A cultural-urban avenue passing through some of the city's most important
institutions and public spaces. High proportion of students, cultural audiences, and
business visitors.
**5. Skenderija — plaza, parking areas, Mirza Delibašić Arena, Dom Mladih (Youth
Center)**
A cultural and sports complex that attracts diverse audiences — from sporting events
to concerts and trade fairs. One of the most spacious locations in the network.
**6. Part of Vilsonovo Šetalište (Wilson's Promenade)**
A popular riverside promenade along the Miljacka River, favored by joggers, families,
and casual visitors. The relaxed atmosphere means people tend to stay longer at
this location.
**7. Bjelašnica — Babin Do**
An Olympic mountain serving as a recreational and tourist destination. Distinctly
seasonal character with high visitor turnover during winter ski season and summer
outdoor season.
### Geographic distribution
The locations span four distinct zones:
- **Historic core**: Baščaršija (Sebilj), Sarači, Ferhadija to Aria Mall
- **Cultural and urban zone**: Zeleni Beretki — Branilaca Sarajeva — Susan Sontag
Square — National Theatre
- **Cultural-sports hub**: Skenderija complex (plaza, parking, Arena, Youth Center)
- **Recreational zones**: Vilsonovo Šetalište and Bjelašnica — Babin Do
### Why these locations?
The selection is deliberate — each point in the network represents an urban node
where tourists, locals, students, and business visitors converge. These are places
where purchasing decisions are made, where leisure time is spent, and where
brands have the greatest chance of being noticed. From the historic center of
Baščaršija to the cultural hub of Skenderija and the natural retreat of Bjelašnica —
Saraya Connect covers the full spectrum of Sarajevo's urban experience.

## 4. The Captive Portal
### What it is
The captive portal is the first page a user sees the moment they connect to the
Saraya Connect WiFi network. It is a mandatory entry point — the user cannot
access the internet without first passing through the portal. This mandatory nature
makes it an exceptionally powerful advertising space.
The portal is accessible at: **https://hs.saraya.solutions**
### How it works (user flow)
1. **User connects** to the Saraya Connect WiFi network at any of the seven
locations.
2. **The portal opens** automatically with a branded "Saraya Connect /
Connecting…" loading screen.
3. **Advertising content is displayed** — the user sees branded videos, banners,
and promotional blocks.
4. **User gets internet access** and can browse freely, while the portal continues to
serve as a content hub.
### Portal design principles
- **Mobile-first**: Optimized for smartphones, since the vast majority of users
connect via mobile devices.
- **Fast loading**: Designed for quick rendering and clean visual presentation.
- **High engagement**: Layout maximizes user interaction with content.
### Portal content sections
Once past the loading screen, users land on a scrollable page with:
- **Hero area** — Rotating video backgrounds and promotional banners with calls to
action. Different content on each visit.
- **Quick Access links** — Horizontal strip of shortcut chips to other Saraya apps
(Explore Sarajevo, Pametno Odabrano, Quiz, Rewards Center, Play & Win).
- **City Services** — Live utility widgets:
- **Weather**: Current conditions, high/low temperatures, and hourly forecast for
Sarajevo.
- **Clock & Date**: Live local time display.
- **Currency Converter**: EUR/USD to BAM (Bosnian Convertible Mark) with live
exchange rates.
- **Saraya Connect Updates** — Scrollable news and announcement cards.
- **Today's Recommended** — Brand and deal tiles with animated text overlays and
promotional offers.
- **Smart Choices** — Curated product picks linking to the Pametno Odabrano
editorial site.
- **Explore Sarajevo** — Place cards linking to local business and attraction listings.
- **Play & Win** — Game hub promo card showing available games.
- **Footer** — Partner names, connectivity status, and links.
### Games page
Accessible from the portal, featuring:
- Filter by difficulty: All, Easy, Medium, Hard.
- Grid of game cards with difficulty labels and estimated play time (2–5 minutes).
- Games open in new browser tabs.
### Language
The portal supports **Bosnian** and **English** via a toggle in the top bar. Default is
English.
### No login required
The Hotspot portal is designed for anonymous WiFi users and works without any
account or login. However, if the user already has an active Saraya session from
logging in on another app, the portal recognizes it silently.
### Role in the ecosystem
The captive portal is the **primary entry point** to the entire Saraya Connect
ecosystem. Many users will first encounter Saraya by connecting to free WiFi. From
the portal, they can discover and navigate to every other Saraya product — Quiz,
Explore Sarajevo, Play & Win, Pametno Odabrano, and the Rewards Center.

## 7. Saraya Accounts (Central Identity & SSO)
### What it is
Saraya Accounts is the **central identity service** for the entire ecosystem. It works
behind the scenes to manage authentication, user profiles, and cross-app session
sharing.
### User registration
- Users create an account with **name, email, and password**.
- Password must be at least **15 characters** long.
- Upon registration, the system automatically creates a coin wallet, an XP profile
(starting at level 1 with 0 XP), and enrolls the account in all platform services (Quiz,
Hotspot, Explore, Rewards Center, Games, Pametno Odabrano, etc.).
- Each new account starts with 0 coins, 0 tokens, 0 XP, and level 1.
### User login
- Users log in with **email and password**.
- After login, the user receives session tokens (access token and refresh token).
- The login response includes the user's full profile: name, email, avatar, coins,
tokens, XP, level, and account status.
- Suspended or deleted accounts cannot log in.
### User profile data
Every user profile contains:
- **Name** (display name)
- **Email**
- **Avatar URL** (optional profile picture)
- **Account status** (Active, Suspended, or Deleted)
- **Coins** (current balance)
- **Tokens** (current balance)
- **XP** (total experience points)
- **Level** (derived from XP)
### How Single Sign-On (SSO) works
SSO allows a user who is logged in on one Saraya app to be automatically logged in
on every other Saraya app without entering credentials again. Here is how it works
from the user's perspective:
1. **User logs in on any Saraya app** (e.g., Saraya Quiz). After login, the app
establishes a shared session cookie on the central accounts domain
(`.saraya.solutions`).
2. **User visits another Saraya app** (e.g., Rewards Center) where they have no
active session. The app silently redirects the user to the central accounts SSO page.
3. **If the user is already authenticated** on the accounts domain, a one-time code is
generated and the user is redirected back to the requesting app with that code.
4. **The requesting app exchanges the code** for session tokens and logs the user
in — no password prompt needed.
5. **If the user is NOT authenticated** on the accounts domain, the app is informed
that login is required, and the user is shown a login screen.
The result: **one account, one login, access to everything.** Users sign in once and
move freely between Quiz, Explore Sarajevo, Pametno Odabrano, Play & Win, and
Rewards Center without re-entering credentials.
### Admin dashboard
Staff members with whitelisted email addresses can access the admin panel to:
- View dashboard metrics: total accounts, suspended accounts, aggregate coin and
token totals across all users.
- Browse and search the full account list.
- Create new accounts (with an initial coin balance).
- Edit any account: change name, email, password, or avatar.
- Adjust balances: add or subtract coins, tokens, or XP for any user.
- Change account status: activate, suspend, or permanently delete accounts (with
confirmation steps).
- Change account status: activate, suspend, or permanently delete accounts (with
confirmation steps).
- View activity history for any account (what they earned, where, and when).
- View a global activity feed of recent events across the platform.

## 8. Saraya Quiz
### What it is
Saraya Quiz is an interactive quiz platform where users answer multiple-choice
questions on various topics and earn Coins and XP for correct answers. Quizzes can
be taken by guests (without an account) or by signed-in users who want to earn
rewards.
### How quizzes work
1. **Browse quizzes** — The home page displays a grid of available quizzes. Each
quiz has a title, description, and a themed visual card.
2. **Start a quiz** — Users choose to play as a signed-in user (to earn rewards) or
as a guest (just for fun, with a display name). Some quizzes require an account and
will redirect to login.
3. **Answer questions** — All questions are multiple choice. Questions and their
answer options are shuffled randomly each time to prevent memorization. All
questions appear on a single scrollable page with a progress bar.
4. **Submit answers** — After answering all questions, the user submits. The
system records the score, time taken, and calculates rewards.
5. **View results** — The results screen shows the score (correct/total), percentage,
and a full review of all answers with the correct answer highlighted for any missed
questions.
### Reward system
- Each quiz has configurable base rewards: **coins, XP, and optionally tokens**.
- **First completion bonus** — The first time a user completes a specific quiz,
rewards are multiplied (e.g., 2x).
- **Perfect score bonus** — Scoring 100% correct triggers an additional multiplier.
- **Daily login streak** — If the user has been claiming daily rewards consistently
(tracked via Rewards Center), a streak multiplier can apply to quiz rewards.
- **Reward attempt limit** — Each quiz has a maximum number of rewarded
completions (default 3). After that, the user can still retake the quiz but will not earn
additional rewards.
- **Guest users** do not earn rewards. The results page prompts them to create an
account to claim earnings.
### Home page features
- Platform statistics: number of active quizzes, total attempts across all users,
completions today.
- Quick toggle between showing a subset or all available quizzes.
- Sign-in / account button with profile information (coins, XP, level, tier).
### User profile (in-app)
When logged in, users can view their profile via a modal that shows:
- Display name and avatar
- Current tier and level
- Coin, token, and XP balances
- A progress bar toward the next level
### Languages
Saraya Quiz is currently available in **English only**.
### Admin features
Quiz administrators (whitelisted emails) can:
- Create, edit, publish, and delete quizzes.
- Configure quiz metadata: title, description, visual theme, icon, and color gradient.
- Add, edit, and remove questions with up to 6 answer choices and a marked correct
answer.
- Set reward parameters: base coins, XP, tokens, first-completion multiplier, perfect-
score multiplier, and max rewarded attempts.
- Set access rules: allow anonymous play, require an account, limit attempts per
device, and set cooldown periods.
- Toggle quizzes between active and inactive states.
- Preview quizzes before publishing.
- Copy shareable quiz links.
- View detailed analytics: per-quiz scores, device/browser/OS breakdowns, recent
attempts, abandoned vs. completed rates, and multi-account-per-device warnings.

## 11. Play & Win (Game Hub)
### What it is
Play & Win is a browser-based mini-game hub where users play casual games and
earn Saraya Coins and XP. It features multiple game types with varying difficulty
levels, daily reward limits to encourage return visits, and integration with the central
Saraya account for rewards.
### Available games
1. **Wheel of Fortune** — Spin a prize wheel to win coins, XP boosts, partner
prizes, jackpots, or "try again." Fully animated spin with a prize reveal modal.
2. **Memory Match** — Classic card-matching game. Choose Easy, Medium, or
Hard difficulty. Cards are previewed briefly, then flipped face-down. Match all pairs
before time runs out.
3. **Puzzle Challenge** — Sliding image puzzle with 3×3, 4×4, or 5×5 grid options.
Drag pieces into the correct positions. Time-based rewards for faster completion.
4. **Word Search** — Find hidden words in a letter grid. Difficulty affects grid size,
word count, and time limit. Select words by dragging across letters.
5. **Pac-Man** — Arcade-style maze game. Difficulty changes ghost count, speed,
and time limits. Eat dots, avoid ghosts, grab power pellets.
6. **Scratch Cards** — Listed as "Coming Soon" on the launcher.
### How rewards work
- **Signed-in users** earn coins and XP when they win or complete games.
- **Daily reward limits**: Each game type has a limit of **3 reward-eligible plays per
day**. For example, users can earn rewards from 3 Wheel of Fortune spins, 3
Memory Match wins, 3 Puzzle completions, etc. — each tracked separately.
- After reaching the daily limit for a game, users can **still play for fun** but will not
earn additional rewards. A banner explains this.
- **Guest users** can play all games but do **not earn any rewards**. They are
prompted to sign in to start earning.
- Reward amounts vary by game, difficulty, and performance (e.g., faster puzzle
completion = more coins).
- Daily limits reset at **midnight CET** (Central European Time).
### Game launcher (home page)

The Rewards Center is the **central marketplace and social hub** of the Saraya
ecosystem. It is where users go to spend their earned coins and tokens on real
rewards, track achievements, compete on leaderboards, connect with friends, chat,
join tournaments, and discover more ways to earn. It has a dark, neon, game-
inspired visual theme.
### Rewards catalog
- Users browse a catalog of redeemable items including **physical products**
(merchandise, gadgets), **digital goods** (codes, subscriptions), and **perks**
(discounts, special access).
- Items are priced in either **Coins** or **Tokens**.

currency (Coins or Tokens), and maximum price.
- Featured and limited-time items are highlighted.
- Announcement banners can appear at the top of the catalog for promotions.

deducted from the user's balance and an order is created.

## 13. Cross-Ecosystem Features & Connections
### Single Sign-On (SSO) — One account for everything
Users create one Saraya account and it works across all apps:
- **Sign up** on any Saraya app (Quiz, Rewards Center, Explore, Pametno, Play &
Win) — the same registration form is used everywhere.
- **Log in once**, and when visiting another Saraya app, the user is automatically
recognized and logged in without any additional action.
- **Profile, balances, and progress sync in real time** across all apps. If a user earns
100 coins on Saraya Quiz, those coins immediately appear in the Rewards Center,
Explore Sarajevo, and everywhere else.
### Unified virtual economy
| Where you earn | How |
|---|---|
| **Saraya Quiz** | Complete quizzes correctly; bonuses for first completion, perfect
scores, and streaks |
| **Explore Sarajevo** | Read about businesses and attractions (20-second
engagement); tiered rewards (50/100/200 coins) |
| **Pametno Odabrano** | Read tech articles (20-second engagement, 50 coins
each); daily login bonus (500 coins) |
| **Play & Win** | Win games (Wheel, Memory, Puzzle, Word Search, Pac-Man);
rewards vary by game and difficulty |
| **Rewards Center** | Daily check-in, streak bonuses, social follows, promo codes,
tournament prizes, referrals |
| **Saraya Connect HS** | No direct earning on the portal itself; it connects users to
earning opportunities on other apps |
| Where you spend | How |
|---|---|
| **Rewards Center** | Browse the catalog and redeem coins/tokens for physical
items, digital goods, and perks |
### Daily limits and streaks
- Most earning activities have **daily caps** to prevent abuse and encourage return
visits:
- Explore Sarajevo: 1,700 coins/day from reading.
- Pametno Odabrano: 1,500 coins/day from reading (30 articles × 50 coins).
- Play & Win: 3 rewarded plays per game type per day.
- Saraya Quiz: configurable max rewarded attempts per quiz.
- Rewards Center: 1 daily check-in per day.
- **Streak system**: Consecutive daily logins/check-ins build a streak that multiplies
rewards. Missing a day resets the streak. Streaks are tracked centrally and the
multiplier can apply across apps.
### Navigation between apps
Every app in the ecosystem provides navigation links to the others:
- **Navigation menus and footers** include links to: Saraya Connect, Rewards
Center, Quiz, Play & Win, Explore Sarajevo, and Pametno Odabrano.
- **Profile dropdowns** (when logged in) show links to other Saraya apps.
- **The Hotspot portal** acts as a central launchpad with quick-access chips to all
apps.
- **The Earn page** in Rewards Center aggregates all earning opportunities with
